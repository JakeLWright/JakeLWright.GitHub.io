Insurance Defense, Business Acquisitions and Corporate Attorneys

Since 1931, the law firm of Hillen, Wicker & Tapscott has delivered comprehensive litigation and corporate legal services to individuals and corporations in Tupelo and throughout Mississippi.

The firm's areas of practice include:
Insurance Defense

Our firm understands insurers' need for streamlined and efficient legal services. We seek creative and novel approaches to insurance defense cases.

Real Estate Law

We represent clients in a wide range of commercial real estate matters from the purchase of raw land, to the sale of developed property and everything in between.

Business Acquisitions and Mergers

We advise business owners, entrepreneurs and managers on business law, start-up assistance including the formation of an entity and on the protection of a trademark or license agreement.

First Amendment and Communications

We offer a full range of services in connection with communications law and First Amendment issues.

Labor and Employment

Our attorneys work closely with employers and human resource professionals to help them understand employment law.

General Civil Litigation

Our civil litigation lawyers represent plaintiffs and defendants including individuals, partnerships, shareholders and corporations.

Bankruptcy

Our law firm assesses how filing bankruptcy can impact your individual situation.

Experienced Legal Council for your business

The firm's attorneys bring decades of experience in the legal field and in business to each case or project they handle for clients, allowing them to intelligently analyze the issues, identify solutions and efficiently resolve even the most complex matter.

The firm is passionate about providing the highest level of legal representation, which not only means delivering sound legal counsel but also personalized service and availability. From the very first consultation, the attorneys take time to understand the client’s unique circumstances, individual needs and future goals. Throughout the legal proceeding, attorneys work with clients, ensuring all of their questions, concerns and issues are addressed.

The attorneys’ unique approach and dedication to their role as both legal counselors and diligent advocates set them apart and has helped the legal team to develop a strong reputation for excellence throughout the area.
